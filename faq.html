FAQ

Do you use organic ingredients?

Yes, whenever possible we use organic and locally sourced items.

Do you cater events?

Absolutely! Contact us for custom catering orders.

Do you offer vegan or gluten-free options?

Yes, we do! At Bakery Bliss, we believe everyone should enjoy a sweet treat. We offer a variety of vegan and gluten-free options daily, including muffins, cookies, and specialty cakes.

How far in advance should I place a custom order?

We recommend placing custom orders at least 3-5 days in advance to ensure we have enough time to create your perfect baked goods.
