About Us

At Bakery Bliss, we believe that every bite should bring a little happiness. Since opening our doors in 2010, we’ve been committed to using only the freshest, locally sourced ingredients to craft delicious breads, cakes, pastries, and more.

From classic favorites to seasonal specials, our baked goods are made with love, tradition, and a pinch of creativity. Whether you're grabbing a morning croissant, ordering a custom birthday cake, or just stopping by for the smell of fresh bread, we’re here to make your day a little sweeter.
